About me

Hi! I’m Rebecca Tong and I’m an aspiring software engineer. I graduated from University at Buffalo with a Bachelor of Arts in Psychology. After graduating college, I had started in retail sales and gradually transitioned into working at the Genius Bar of Apple. I had started Flatiron’s Fullstack Web Development Program and enjoyed troubleshooting code and building applications. Utilizing the skills that I’ve learned in the Genius Bar, I developed a knack for troubleshooting and finding the solution for the user. I was able to enhance my troubleshooting skills with code and in user scenarios. Aside from software developement, I enjoy writing short stories in my blog - https://medium.com/@rbcatong.
